Obiter

AGLC4, applied automatically.

A free, open-source Microsoft Word add-in that handles AGLC4 citation formatting so you can focus on your argument. Insert footnotes, generate bibliographies, and validate references — all without leaving your document.

From obiter dictum — a remark in passing. Citations are the remarks that support the argument. Also sounds like “oh, better” — which is what your citations become.

Download from AppSource View on GitHub

What Obiter Does

Comprehensive AGLC4 citation support, built into the tool you already use.

A4

Full AGLC4 Coverage

Cases, legislation, treaties, journal articles, books, and more. Every source type formatted to the standard, every time.

»

Automatic Ibid

Obiter detects repeated sources and applies ibid automatically, following the strict single-source rule in AGLC4 1.4.3.

§

Cross-references

Short titles, above/below notes, and pinpoint references are managed and updated as your document evolves.

B

Bibliography Generation

Generate a correctly ordered and formatted AGLC4 bibliography from your citations in one step.

✓

Document Validation

Scan your entire document for formatting issues, broken references, and missing fields before you submit.

AI

LLM Integration

Optional AI-assisted citation lookup and field extraction, reducing manual data entry for unfamiliar sources.

☉

15+ Foreign Jurisdictions

Coverage extends beyond Australian materials to international courts, the United Kingdom, the United States, Canada, New Zealand, and more.

5

AGLC5 Ready

Architectured to support future editions. When AGLC5 arrives, Obiter will be updated to match the new standard.

How It Works

Three steps from installation to your first properly formatted citation.

1

Install

Add Obiter to Microsoft Word from AppSource. It appears as a task pane alongside your document, ready when you are.

2

Insert Citation

Select a source type, fill in the fields, and click Insert. Obiter formats the footnote and inserts it at your cursor position.

3

Write

Continue writing. Obiter manages ibid, cross-references, and short titles as your document grows. Generate a bibliography when you are done.
